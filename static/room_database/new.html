Lovely large rooms, each with their own kitchenette (without hobs) and ensuite. All are grade 8's so not the cheapest option, however they are in good condition. One potential downfall is that you will have to completely empty rooms in the holidays as they are rented out by college. Very central in college but could be lonely/isolated if you're there without any friends nearby.
