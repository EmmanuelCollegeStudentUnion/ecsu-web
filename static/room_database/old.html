Old Court

Go Back a Page

Great place to live if you want a large living room shared with a best mate. Rooms vary and some are more slanty than others, have a look round before you decide. Toilets and showers have been in South Court in past years however in 2014 college are installing bathrooms on the third floor, so bear this in mind.

The ballot for the old court sets (not including the single rooms on the 3rd floor) is done separately to the rest of the ballot. You join into a pair with your friend and your positions in the original ballot get added together to form a new score. If you're unsuccessful in getting a room in the old court ballot then you'll go back into the normal one in your original position.
