North Court

Go Back a Page

North Court comprises of 7 individual staircases in older, more aesthetically pleasing buildings.

Staircases Y, Z and Cloisters have been recently refurbished and have card access and hobs available for cooking.

Reasonably near everything in college, and right by the self service laundry machines, however you do have to deal with the hobbit hole for getting bikes in and out. Overall a nice place to live if you have friends there too.
