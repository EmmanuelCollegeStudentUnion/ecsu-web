Hostel

Hostel has access to hobs in all the kitchens. It has varying size rooms and some suffer from noise from buses on the road behind college (however they all have double glazing so it's not too much of a problem). The rooms overlooking paddock are the nicest, but they are mostly a grade higher accordingly. Shower and toilets are all in the basement, however most rooms have a sink and there are gyp rooms on most/all floors. Very close to laundry
