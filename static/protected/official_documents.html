Freedom of Information Act

Although ECSU isn't obliged to provide a publication scheme under the provisions of the Freedom of Information Act, we see the Act as a guide for good practice. As a result, all our official documents, including minutes of Exec and Open Meetings, are available to download.

About Us

ECSU is the Emmanuel College Students' Union, which is the representative body for students of Emmanuel College, Cambridge. ECSU provides a bar, common room and funding for sports and other societies, as well as acting as the channel of communication between the students and the College.
ECSU is operated by an Executive Committee, elected on an annual basis.
