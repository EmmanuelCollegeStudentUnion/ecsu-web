Section 4: Comments

We haven't had new kit for the girls team for a number of years. Currently, we play in an assortment of pink t-shirts of varying shades. This regularly causes confusion with other teams, and means that playing mixed matches (such as in our current cuppers campaign) that we do not match the boys, or even eachother when playing so often get confused when passing the ball, so limiting our performance.

We would also like to be able to provide kit (on a borrow each week and return) basis to new players (particularly freshers) as we feel this will eliminate an element of apprehension about playing hockey for the college (as one wouldn't have to find their own shirt). We would plan that the captain would distribute shirts each match and collect them at the end to wash before the next week's match.

We would like new goalie shorts because they are broken, and becoming dangerous to play in. We haven't had new goalie kit for a very long time (before our time).
